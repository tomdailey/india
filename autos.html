Autos

July 23, 2019

When there's as many people as there are here, its worth it to have a smaller vehicle! Hence these cute little taxis they call "autos". It's like riding on a golf cart, except you don't have to golf (*sigh of relief*).

Also, look carefully at the photo above. There an extra taste of India in there for you.

Seriously these things are everywhere. If you cough and it sounds even vaguely of the word "auto", three drivers will immediately surround you asking where you want to go.
