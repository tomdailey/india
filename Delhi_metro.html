The gorgeous Delhi metro

Publicly funded luxury, July 22 2019

See how happy he is? Why wouldn't you be on this lovely metro?

The AC is real-- and its all sooo clean! Hands down the best place to be come mid-afternoon. You can cool off (maybe even better-- dry off), and enjoy some good old fashioned people watching. No loud performances, no rats-- suck it NYC.
