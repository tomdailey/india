An Ethiopian Treat

July 21, 2019

Solomon is a chef back home, and came with some fhet for exactly this occasion. Fhet is a grain native to Ethiopia, with which this sort of bubbly peta is made. He then cooked chunks of said peta into this sour-spicy combo. Very tasty.

He and Hanna just finished their first of five Vipassana mediation retreats they'll do while in the country. Vipassana is a mediation practice revolving around "mindfulness." The retreats entail varying degrees of isolation/bans on communication of any sort. Business idea: Vipassana retreats for high powered executives-- they pay thousands and you just give em a bed and some rice. Call it "Scarcity Meditation" or something.
