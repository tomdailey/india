Hyderabad

July 29, 2019

Up above the fray of the screaming city. Feels good.

The city is next level crazy, and pretty dirty, and very loud. It's the five-year-old-me's equivalent of hell on earth. But I managed to find a place above it, and I think it's growing on me.
